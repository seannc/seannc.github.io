Fun Facts


With 81 million people Germany has the largest population in the European Union.

The name “Germany”” comes from the Latin Germania, the Roman name for the lands north of the Alps, where the Barbarian tribes lived.

There are over 1,500 kinds of sausages made in Germany and over 300 kinds of bread.

In Bavaria, beer is officially defined as staple food, like bread, and not as alcohol.
