Fun Facts


The Kingdom of Spain, as it’s officially known is the second largest country in the European Union.

Having free time and siestas (naps) are a huge part of everyday Spanish culture.

Nearly three quarters of the world’s rice colouring ingredient, Saffron is grown Spain.

Spain is known for many dishes such as the original paella. Widely known for being a seafood dish it is also sometimes cooked with chicken, rabbit and pork (and sometimes snails).
