Fun Facts


Russia is the largest country on earth, with a total area of 17,075,400 square kilometers.

Moscow has more billionaire residents than any other city in the world.

The word "vodka" derives for the word "voda", which means "water".

Moscow's Fallen Monument Park has a bunch of unwanted Soviet statues.

Russia has 11 time zones, more than any other country.

Russia has both a president and a prime minister.
